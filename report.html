ST12-ICD/ABT-096

Abotilla Incident

A containment breach involving SCP-096 originated at Site-██ due to unauthorized imaging. The file propagated outside Foundation systems, leading to civilian exposure.

Incident

SCP-096 entered Abotilla within minutes, eliminating exposed individuals. Panic and environmental damage caused additional casualties.

Response

Mobile Task Force Eta-10 ("See No Evil") contained the situation using non-visual methods.

Survivors

19 individuals survived. All were treated with amnestics and relocated.

Public Cover

The incident was officially reported as a gas explosion.
