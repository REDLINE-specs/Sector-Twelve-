FILE: ST12-ICD/ABT-096

INCIDENT: ABOTILLA EVENT

Origin Site: Site-██

Entity: SCP-096

Classification: EUCLID

Summary:

A containment breach involving SCP-096 occurred following unauthorized visual exposure during a routine inspection. A partial image of the entity’s face was captured and transmitted beyond Foundation-secured systems.

Incident Development:

The image reached civilian networks and was accessed by multiple individuals located in the village of Abotilla. SCP-096 entered an active state shortly thereafter and began tracking exposed individuals.

The entity arrived at Abotilla within approximately eight minutes. Termination of exposed subjects was immediate and absolute. Secondary casualties occurred due to structural collapse and civilian panic.

Response:

Mobile Task Force Eta-10 (“See No Evil”) established perimeter containment. No direct visual contact protocols were maintained. Entity activity ceased after completion of its termination cycle.

Survivors:

Nineteen (19) individuals survived due to lack of exposure. Survivors displayed acute psychological trauma and fragmented recollection. Class-B amnestics were administered. All individuals were relocated and reassigned identities.

Public Narrative:

The incident was publicly reported as a gas pipeline explosion followed by structural fire. The area was declared hazardous and subsequently cleared.
